Ancient Puzzles

The earliest puzzles date back to ancient civilizations, such as the Egyptian riddles and Chinese tangrams.

The Rise of Jigsaw Puzzles

In the 18th century, John Spilsbury, a British cartographer, created the first jigsaw puzzle by cutting a wooden map into pieces.

Modern-Day Puzzles

Today, puzzles have expanded into digital formats, including online brain games and AI-generated riddles.
